Rodan is developed by the Distributed Digital Music Archives Libraries (DDMAL) lab in McGill university. DDMAL is a part of the Single Interface for Music Score Searching and Analysis (SIMSSA) team which is divided into two axes: Content and Analysis. Rodan brings these axes together.

Feedback

If you find Rodan useful and are using it in any deployments (large or small), please let us know. This work is part of our research, and keeping in touch about pilot projects or full-scale deployments helps us figure out what kinds of tools are valuable in digital library communities.
